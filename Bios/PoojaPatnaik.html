Pooja Patnaik, MD

Neurology Resident Physician, 2024-Present

My research focuses on improving our ability to predict outcomes and guide personalized treatment for patients with central-nervous system lymphoma (CNSL) using cfDNA sequencing, also known as "liquid biopsy." I am specifically developing a targeted cfDNA sequencing assay to evaluate minimal residual disease and track immune dynamics in patients with relapsed/refractory CNSL receiving anti-CD19 chimeric antigen receptor T-cell (CART) therapy. My long-term goal is to apply this to larger cohorts to establish biomarkers of resistance to immunotherapy that could guide personized therapeutics as well as clinical trial design and implementation.
