Murakami Lab

Purpose

The Murakami Lab at the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute in Boston, MA studies minimal residual disease in B-cell leukemia and lymphoma and aims to translate mechanistic discoveries into individualized therapies to improve outcomes in patients with these diseases.
