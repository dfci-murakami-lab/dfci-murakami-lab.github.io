Purpose

Murakami Lab

The Murakami Lab at the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute in Boston, MA studies minimal residual disease in B-cell leukemia and lymphoma and aims to translate mechanistic discoveries into individualized therapies to improve outcomes in patients with these diseases by utilizing our repository of preclinical models including patient-derived xenograft (PDX) mouse models, access to conventional and experimental therapeutic agents, expertise in lymphoid genomics and bioinformatics, novel single-cell biophysical and molecular profiling platforms, and involvement in early phase clinical trials.
